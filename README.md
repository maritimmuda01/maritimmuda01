# Welcome To Maritim Muda Nusantara

Maritim Muda Nusantara (Indonesian Maritime Youths) commonly called Maritim Muda, is a national youth organization in the maritime field in the form of an association (association). Maritim Muda was formed on December 13 2018 to coincide with Nusantara Day in Jakarta by 6 young Indonesians whose hearts and souls are called to serve the homeland and nation through sustainable maritime development, namely the Late Emperor, Antonius Rio Sandi Sirait, Imanda Hikmat Pradana, Ranitya Nurlita, Denny Suwarso Putra, and Bondan Bhaskara.
